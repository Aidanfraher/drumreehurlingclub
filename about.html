About Drumree Hurling and Camogie Club

Drumree Hurling and Camogie Club has a proud tradition of promoting GAA sports in the Meath area. Founded over a century ago, the club continues to nurture talent, foster team spirit, and contribute to our vibrant community.

Our facilities include a state-of-the-art pitch, clubhouse, and training grounds. We welcome players of all ages and skill levels to join us in celebrating the rich heritage of hurling and camogie.
